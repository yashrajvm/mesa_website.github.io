Badminton Rules

  • Registration is open only for Mechanical Engineering students and department faculty.
  • Only doubles tournament will be held.
  • Participants have to bring their own rackets.
  • All matches will follow the knockout format.
  • In case of tie/odd number of teams left at the end, advantage will be given to the team with maximum point difference.
  • Matches will be of one set with 21 points.
  • Maximum registration:
    • B.tech Mechanical: 6 X 4 =24 teams.
    • M.tech Mechanical: 7 X 2=14 teams.
    • Phd + Faculty (Mechanical): 10 teams.

Cricket Rules

  • A team will consist of 8 player.
  • All matches will follow the knockout format.
  • Matches will be held at Athletics ground.
  • Red Tennis ball will be used in matches.
  • An inning will be of 8 overs.
  • Maximum registration:
    • B.tech Mechanical: 6 teams (Maximum 2 teams from a particular year).
    • M.tech Mechanical: 2 X 2=4 teams.
    • Phd + Faculty (Mechanical): 2 teams.
