Alumni Talks



Saurav Mohanty  

Graduated with Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering in 2016 from IIT Guwahati, Saurav Mohanty joined Boston Consulting Group as an Associate. Fast forward 3 years and with an experience as a Senior Associate for 1 year, he currently is working as a full-time Consultant for the past 6 months. His time spent in IIT Guwahati is no less exciting than his journey after college into the world of consultancy. Apart from excelling in academics, he has headed the Finance and Economics Club, has been the Internship Coordinator and also a member of the Placement Cell. He has dabbled in various extracurricular activities and we can't wait to hear about all the exciting experiences he has to share with us. So mark your calendars and don't miss this chance to interact with one of the very successful alumni of IIT Guwahati and know more about the opportunities the real world has to present.
"Watch the best. See what they did right. Observe their journey and learn. Because everyone has a story to tell and it is worth listening" -Satya Nadella (CEO, Microsoft)





Lecture on Industry 4.0

A lecture on Industry 4.0 will be delivered by Dr. Deepak Sharma, Associate Professor at IIT Guwahati. Dr. Sharma's research interests are in the field of optimization and Soft Computing Techniques for Intelligent Design and Manufacturing.Industry 4.0 is the subset of the fourth industrial revolution that concerns industry. The fourth industrial revolution encompasses areas which are not normally classified as an industry, such as smart cities, for instance.
