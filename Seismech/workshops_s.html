3-D Printing

  • You will learn:-
    • 3-d Printing Basics.
    • Introduction to Cura an open source 3D printer slicing application.
    • Building a 3-d model straight from G-code.
  • A Certificate of Completion will be awarded to all the participants at the end of workshop.
  • Software Requirements:-
    • Cura installed on your laptop.
  • Venue for the workshop will be Conference Hall near Auditorium.
  • It will be a five hour long workshop including a small tea break. The event will start at 9 'o' clock in the morning of 15th March.
  • The workshop will be conducted by Dr. Sajan Kapil, Assistant Professor at IIT Guwahati.

Python for Data Science

  • You will learn:-
    • Python Language Basics.
    • Introduction to various libraries such as Numpy, Pandas, Scipy, Matplotlib and Seaborn.
    • A hands-on mini project.
  • A Certificate of Completion will be awarded to all the participants at the end of workshop.
  • Software Requirements:-
    • Python 3 and Jupyter notebook installed on your laptop.
    • Preinstalled Libraries- Numpy, Pandas, Scipy, Matplotlib and Seaborn.
  • Venue for the workshop will be Conference Hall near Auditorium.
  • It will be a five hour long workshop including a small tea break. The event will start at 1 'o' clock in the afternoon of 14th March.
  • The workshop will be conducted by Mr. Akshay Parekh a research scholar at IIT Guwahati currently conducting his research on Natural Language Processing.

Matlab

  • Venue for the workshop will be Conference Hall near Auditorium.
