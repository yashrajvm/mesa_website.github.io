MESA ORIENTATION

In order to welcome the fresher undergraduates and graduates to the MESA family and make them aware about the constitution and the working of MESA, an orientation was organized on 20th August, 2016. They were also introduced to Seismech, the departmental symposium that is held annually. Various suggestion were also taken for the future events that should be organized by MESA. The freshers who wanted to work for MESA, were asked to step forward. A fun introduction session took place, in which many of them revealed their true personalities. It was followed by a Quiz for the freshers, based on various general topics in Mechanical Engineering.

Intern Talk

Intern Talk

To learn from the internship experiences of our seniors and to know more about the do’s and don’ts while applying and going for an intern, MESA organized an Internship talk with our seniors. The session was organized separately for the second and third years owing to the different problems faced by them.

Farewell Party

Farewell Party

Last semester MESA organized farewell party for the 4th year senior, 2016 batch as a token of gratitude for their support and guidance during these 4 years. The party was enjoyed a lot by the seniors who recited the old memories of the 4 years in IIT Guwahati. The event even saw performances by some of them. This was followed by special dinner for them.
